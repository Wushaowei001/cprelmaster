Detailed Description

The propagators presented in this module extend common operations on relations to constraints. As opposed to databases, columns in the relation decission variables are identified by their possition. This is, a relation of arity n has columns \(0,\ldots,n-1\) being the column identified with 0 the right most one.

As the notion of "colum names" is merely numeric, some of the constraints that work with column information take numbers identifying the columns as parameters. Moreover, constraints like join and projection rely on some conventions regarding this imput.
