Main propagation algorithm.

Implements: \(\Pi_{p}A=B\)

Where:

  • \(A \subseteq \mathcal{U}_{n}\)
  • \(p \in \{x : 0 \leq x \leq n\}\)
  • \(B \subseteq \mathcal{U}_{p}\)

Conventions:

  • \(\mathcal{U}_{n}\) denotes the universe of all the tuples with arity \(n\)
  • \(A_{\text{glb}} = \text{glb}(A)\)
  • \(A_{\text{lub}} = \text{lub}(A)\)
  • \(\Pi_{p}^{*}R\) denotes the projection operation on ground relation \(R\)

Propagation rules:

  • \(\Pi_{p}^{*}A_{\text{glb}} \subseteq B \subseteq \Pi_{p}^{*}A_{\text{lub}}\)
  • \((U_{n-p}\times B_{\text{lub}}) \cap A_{\text{lub}} \supseteq A\)
  • \(A \subseteq U_{q}\cap (U_{n-p}\times B_{\text{glb}})\)

Where:

  • \(q \subseteq \{x:p\leq x \leq n-1\}\)
  • \(U_{q} = A_{\text{lub}}\cap\exists !_{q}A_{\text{lub}}\)
